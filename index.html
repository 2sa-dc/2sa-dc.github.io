What We Do

Statistical Consulting
for High-Stakes Decisions

From experimental design through final analysis, we partner with research teams and agencies to ensure scientific rigor at every step.

Experimental Design

Custom designs for complex biological, agricultural, and environmental studies—including split-plot, repeated measures, and adaptive protocols for small-sample research.

Advanced Statistical Modeling

Mixed models, GLMs, Bayesian methods, multivariate analysis, and machine learning tailored to high-variability data with rigorous interpretation and reproducibility.

Bioinformatics & Genomics

Statistical support for genomics pipelines, gene expression analysis, and multi-omics integration—bridging biology and data science.

Statistical Analysis Plans

Structured, pre-registered analysis plans for regulatory submissions, multi-year studies, and strategic research portfolios that withstand peer review.

Training & Capacity Building

Workshops and mentorship on statistical methodology, software tools, and data literacy—tailored for researchers, analysts, and program managers.

Peer Review & QA

Independent statistical review of manuscripts, proposals, and research plans—ensuring analytical integrity before submission or publication.

Who We Are

Built on Federal Research.
Driven by Scientific Rigor.

A boutique team of PhD-level statisticians with deep roots in government science.

2 Steps Ahead Data Consulting was founded by Dr. Kathleen M. Yeater, a biostatistician with over 20 years as Area Statistician for the USDA Agricultural Research Service. During that tenure, she supported more than 150 principal investigators across 22 research locations—shaping experimental design, modeling strategy, and statistical interpretation for some of the nation's most critical agricultural and environmental research.

Kathleen holds a PhD in Applied Statistics from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, with additional graduate work in agronomy and cytogenetics. This intersection of quantitative depth and domain expertise defines the firm's approach: we don't just run models—we understand the science behind the data.

Our team brings the same rigor and collaborative discipline to every engagement, whether it's a focused experimental design sprint or a multi-year analytical partnership.
